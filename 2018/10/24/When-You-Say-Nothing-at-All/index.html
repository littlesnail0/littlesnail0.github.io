When You Say Nothing at All

2018.10.24 little bear  热度 ℃

It’a amazing how you can speak right to my heart
真令人惊奇，你竟能一语说中我的心思
without saying a word you can light up the dark
无需任何话语，你就能照亮这星空
try as i may i can never explain
我尝试过，可还是无法解释明白
what i hear when you don’t say a thing
每当你沉默无语时我听见的是什么
the smile you on your face lets me know that you need me
你脸上的笑容使我明白你需要我
there’s a truth in your eyes saying you’ll never leave me
你眼中的依恋使我知道你不会离我而去
the tought of your hand says you’ll catch me wherever i fall
双手的触摸告诉我无论我在何处跌倒,你都会搀扶我
you say it best when you say nothing at all
你说那种感觉真好，一切尽在不言中
all day long i can hear people talking out loud
一整天，我都在听人们高谈阔论
but when you hold me near you drown the crowd
但当你拥着我离开人群
Old Mr. Webster could never define
无论他们如何努力也永远不会明白
what’s been said between your heart and mine
我俩默契的心中到底承诺了些什么
